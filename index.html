Section 01 · The Faculty第 01 节 · 这项能力

What Is Spatial Intelligence空间智能什么是空间智能

Spatial intelligence is the mind's power to build, hold, and manipulate objects that have no physical form — to rotate a machine in the dark of the skull, to watch gears turn that were never cast. It is the workshop where every invention is first assembled.空间智能，是心灵建造、保持并操控「没有物理形态之物」的能力——在颅骨的黑暗中旋转一台机器，看着从未被铸造过的齿轮转动。它，是每一项发明最初被组装起来的工坊。

Same, or Mirror?相同，还是镜像？

Mentally rotate the right object to see if it can be made to match the left. If it can — they are the SAME. If no rotation will ever align them — it is a MIRROR.在心中旋转右侧物体，看它能否与左侧重合。若能，它们便「相同」；若任何旋转都无法使其对齐，那它便是「镜像」。

When you imagine, your brain reuses the very machinery of perception. To picture a rotating cube is to drive the visual and parietal cortex as if you were truly seeing it. Imagination is perception, run in reverse.当你想象时，大脑重新调用了知觉本身的机器。在脑中描绘一个旋转的立方体，就是在驱动视觉皮层与顶叶皮层——仿佛你真的看见了它。想象，是被反向运行的知觉。
